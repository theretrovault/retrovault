## Long project description

RetroVault is a self-hosted retro game collection manager designed for serious collectors, flippers, and deal hunters. It brings together collection inventory, market pricing, Field Mode for live buying decisions, watchlists, P&L tracking, sourcing workflows, and operator-friendly backup/restore discipline in a single app. The goal is simple: stop using a spreadsheet that knows nothing, and start using a vault that actually helps you buy smarter, sell smarter, and understand your collection.

---

## Why RetroVault exists

Most collection tools are good at cataloging, but weak at the real-world workflow of retro collecting: checking prices in the field, spotting duplicates, tracking what you actually paid, understanding margins, and keeping your own data under your control. RetroVault exists to close that gap.

---

## Self-hosted value block

Your collection data stays under your control. RetroVault is self-hosted, open source, and designed so install, upgrade, backup, and restore are operator-friendly instead of scary.
