# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

RetroVault is a project for retro gaming enthusiasts. We're committed to making this a welcoming space for everyone — experienced collectors, new hunters, developers, and anyone who loves games.

## Our Standards

**Be excellent to each other:**
- Respectful, helpful discussion
- Constructive feedback on contributions
- Welcoming to people at all experience levels
- Patient with questions that seem basic

**Not welcome:**
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind
- Gatekeeping ("you're not a real collector if...")
- Spam or off-topic promotion
- Dishonest pricing advice or market manipulation discussion

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in GitHub Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other RetroVault community spaces.

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported to the project maintainer. Reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately. Violations may result in removal from the community.
